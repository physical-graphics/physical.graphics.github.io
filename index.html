Augmented Reality Invisible A-Pillar

Embedded C++ development with eye tracking to reveal hazards

The pillars either side of the windshield block a significant part of the driver's view.

This solution uses ray tracing from the tracked driver's eyes to crop and transform the feed from an external camera.

Running at 45Hz with a bespoke eye-tracking method for robustness, this felt really wonderful when driving.
